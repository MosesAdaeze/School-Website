Design Thinking

User research is central to UX design. when you engage users and understand their experience, you can move
from being aware of a problem to understanding why the problem exists and what users want to resolved.
There are many different user research tools; this section will explore the most effective of them.
Topics include: user research methods, research plans, recruiting users through screener surveys, diary studies,
and intrviews.
In this UCD discovery unit, you'll work on some mini-projects to shaepen your research skills and then apply
those skills to your capstone projects.
